# Co-author asks

How to write a request that costs a co-author almost nothing.

A weak ask is `please review the chapter`. It stalls because it is unbounded. A strong ask names one concrete thing, shows the surrounding prose is already written, and points at the one slot where the contribution drops in.

Template.
- what is needed, in one sentence
- the exact form (a series, a number, two sentences, one example)
- the slot, shown as the text immediately before and after the gap
- who it is for (owner, or TODO confirm)
- why only they can do it (it is Croatian or domestic, per the reserved zone)

Example.
The box in chapter 14 needs one Croatian fiscal series, general government expenditure as a share of GDP, from 2015 to 2023, plus two sentences of context. The paragraphs around it are written. It drops in between the paragraph ending `na razini Europske unije` and the paragraph beginning `Takav raspored`. For Milan.

One ask, one action. Never bundle several into one message.
